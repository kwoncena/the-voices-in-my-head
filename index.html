The voices in my head, they softly speak,

Whispering secrets in shadows bleak.

They weave through dreams, both night & day,

They guide my steps in their cryptic way.



Echoes of past, and futures unseen,

In the labyrnth of my mind, they convene.

Some are gentle, like a soothing breeze,

While others roar like tempestuous seas.



They sing of love, of hope, of fears,

Echoing laughter, shedding tears.

A chorus of chaos, a symphony of strife

Navigating the complexities of life.



They offer counsel, both wise and wild,

As I journey through the darkness, exiled.

Yet admist the tumult, I find solace deep

In the whispered widsom they softly keep.



For the voices in my head, though they may confound

Are the echoes of my soul, profound.

Guiding me through the labrynth within,

To find the truth, to conquer, to win.
